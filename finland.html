Facts about Finland:
  • Finnish parliament is called 'Eduskunta', and it consists of 200 seats
  • Last election to Eduskunta was held on April 2, 2023, and the next one is expected in 2027
  • Finland elects 15 representatives to the EU Parliament
  • Finnish word for 'party' is 'puolue'
