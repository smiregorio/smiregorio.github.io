Facts about Estonia:
  • Estonian parliament is called 'Riigikogu', and it is consisted of 101 seats
  • Last election to Riigikogu was held on March 5, 2023, and the next election is expected in 2027
  • Estonia elects 7 representatives to the EU Parliament
  • Estonian word for 'party' is 'erakond'
Table Comments:
  • Columns 'National Mandates', 'Government Status', and 'EU mandates' reflect changes after the respective election if occurred
  • For opposition parties, the value 'major' refers to the party with the highest number of national mandates
  • For government parties, the value 'major' refers to the prime minister party
  • Estonian Conservative People's Party and Party 'Fatherland' share the status of the biggest opposition party because they have the same number of seats in the parliament
