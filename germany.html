Facts about Germany:
  • German parliament is called 'Bundestag', and the number of its members is consisted due to the mixed electoral system, though it is reformed to limit the size to 630 seats from the next election and on. This topic would be covered in a separate page. At the moment it is consisted of 733 deputies
  • Last election to Bundestag was held on September 26, 2021, and the next one is expected in February, 2025
  • Germany elects 96 representatives to the EU Parliament (the highest number)
  • German word for party is 'Partei'
Table Comments:
  • Columns 'National Mandates','Government Status', and 'EU mandates' reflect changes after the respective election if occurred
  • For opposition parties, the value 'major' refer to the party with the highest number of national mandates
  • For government parties, the value 'major' refer to the prime minister party
  • Christian Democratic Union of Germany and Christian Social Union in Bavaria share the status of the biggest opposition party because they comprise one faction in the parliament
