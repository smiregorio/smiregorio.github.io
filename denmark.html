Facts about Denmark:
  • Danish parliament is called 'Folketing', and it consists of 179 seats
  • Last election to Folketing was held on November 1, 2022, and the next one is expected in 2026
  • Denmark elects 15 representatives to the EU Parliament
  • Danish word for party is 'parti'
Table Comments:
  • Columns 'National Mandates','Government Status', and 'EU mandates' reflect changes after the respective election if occurred
  • For opposition parties, the value 'major' is refered to the party with the highest number of national mandates
  • For government parties, the value 'major' is refered to the prime minister party
  • The value 'government - support' refer to the party that does not hold any portfolios in the cabinet, but, since the government lacks majority in the parliament, it relies on the votes of this party
